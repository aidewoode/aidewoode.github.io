Guitar tuner for all your needs.

DropD is a fantastic and powerful guitar tuner app with minimal design that help you tune your guitar more easily.

Included features:

  • Strobe mode.
  • Chromatic mode.
  • Multiple tuning options.
  • Create custom tuning.
  • Sync custom tuning with iCloud.
  • Dark mode.
  • And more.
